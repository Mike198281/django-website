About Mike

Hello! I'm Michael Plemmons, the founder and lead bookkeeper here at Plemmons Bookkeeping Services. With a strong foundation in both technology and finance, I hold bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and Accounting, which allows me to offer a unique approach to managing your business's finances.

Based in Weed, California, I bring a blend of precision, problem-solving skills, and a passion for helping small businesses thrive. As a single father raising my daughter, I understand the importance of balance—whether in life or in your books. I work diligently to ensure that your finances are accurate, organized, and ready to support your business goals.

When I'm not crunching numbers or optimizing financial systems, you can find me playing guitar—one of my favorite ways to unwind. Whether you're a startup, a growing business, or simply need reliable bookkeeping services, I’m here to help you navigate the financial side of your business with confidence.
